Our Mission & Vision

Mission

CABINET Hub Zinder seeks to ameliorate Youth Entrepreneurial Skills in Zinder Niger by providing young Nigeriens aged 18-45 with Education, Empowerment, Engagement and Employment within a conducive co-working environment.

Vision

We aim to serve around 40,000 youth who have acquired 10 years of basic literacy skills within the formal school system

Digital Skills Training

We are providing the the local youth with digital opportunities

The digital empowerment program at Zinder is equiping the youth, especially women and girls, with digital skills that will enable them to improve their socio-economic status, become change agents, and make a positive impact on their communities. By bridging the digital divide, the program has contributed to the development of the city and creation of a sustainable future for its inhabitants.

Enterpreneurship Trainings

Cabinet Zinder is empowering the people of Niger by providing them with the tools and knowledge they need to succeed as entrepreneurs. Through our comprehensive entrepreneurship training program, we are unlocking the potential of individuals, especially those in underserved communities, and creating opportunities for them to build sustainable businesses. By equipping our participants with skills in business planning, marketing, financial management, and more, we are catalyzing economic growth and promoting self-reliance in Niger. Join us in transforming lives and communities through entrepreneurship.

Hackathon

We organize and participate in Hackathons

This year we participated in United Nations Economic Commision for Africa Hackathon on leveraging digital technologies for climate change and adaptation in February 2023, Niamey Niger.

Cabinet hub have eqully being organizing, hosting and facilitating worshops across Nigeria and Niger in addition to providing mentorship and learning resources to participants. these hackathons are centered around solving critical problems that our communities are facing locally around Nigeria and Niger republic.

Partners & Networks

Meet our lead Partner: Bank Of Africa Foundation

Through our partnership with Bank Of Africa Foundation we are improving the lives of vulnerable youth by providing them with access to quality education and network through digital skills empowerment.

American Corner (American Space) Zinder is our strategic partner in implementation of programs that improve youth participation in leadership and decision making in areas that affect their lives and communities.

We are proud members of AfriLabs which is a network organisation supporting Innovation Centers across African countries since 2011, founded upon the mission of building a community around the rapidly emerging technology hubs in Africa.
